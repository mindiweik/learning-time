Rigby

~ The Whiny Little Brother ~

HOME | ROSIE | FAVORITE THINGS



  • Age: 2.5 years
  • Breed: Mini Australian Shepherd
  • Coloration: Black Tri
  • Neutered Male
  • Born February 28, 2018

Rigby was born in a home in Pueblo, Colorado where he was raised for the first 9 weeks of his life with both of his parents and his siblings. His new family came to see him and his final remaining brother. Rigby (originally named "Andrew") stole the heart of his new parents and his sister was tolerant enough that it was determined he would fit the best with his new family.

Rigby is a rambunctious little guy. He loves to play fetch and to run around playing chase with other dogs, particularly his sister. He is more wary of people, but he absolutely adores other dogs. His favorite days are when he gets to go to the dog park or the doggie day care he sometimes frequents. No matter what, he just wants to run around and be his normal happy self!
